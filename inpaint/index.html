局部重绘（Inpainting）只重画你圈出的区域，其余像素保持不变——适合换物体、修瑕疵、改局部细节。GPT-Image-2 Tools 内置遮罩画笔（画笔/橡皮/撤销），纯客户端运行，密钥只存本机。

Inpainting repaints only the region you mark while keeping the rest intact — perfect for swapping objects, fixing flaws or changing local details. GPT-Image-2 Tools ships a built-in mask brush (paint / erase / undo), runs fully client-side, and keeps your key on your device.

常见问题 · FAQ

局部重绘会动到其他区域吗？

只重绘你涂出的遮罩区域，未涂的部分尽量保持原样。

Will it change the rest of the image? Only the masked area you paint is repainted; the unmasked parts are kept as-is.

涂错了能撤销吗？

可以，遮罩画笔支持橡皮和撤销，随时调整选区。

Can I undo a wrong stroke? Yes — the mask brush supports erase and undo so you can refine the selection anytime.

需要单独的 inpaint 模型吗？

用你配置的 OpenAI 兼容图像端点即可，工具会带上遮罩发起编辑请求。

Do I need a special inpaint model? Your configured OpenAI-compatible image endpoint is enough; the tool sends the mask with the edit request.
